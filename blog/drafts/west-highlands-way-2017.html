West Highlands Way 2017

  • So 13 August 2017
  • misc
  • #Sports, #Edinburgh

Two friends of mine and me decided to walk the West Highlands Way in Scotland as I am studying in Edinburgh for my Master’s degree. Being totally new to hiking, what could go wrong with hiking the 150km.

The West Highlands Way in Milngavie which is not far from Glasgow and ends in Fort Williams. The route is shown in the following picture
