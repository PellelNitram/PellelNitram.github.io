Introduction

Fluids take a pervasive role for every create on earth: From the daily changing weather over biological liquids inside our bodies down to the inner working of planets. There is a broad range of different fluids that are categorised into so called Newtonian and non-Newtonian fluids, which differ in how the fluid responds to stresses. Liquids and gases such as water and air belong to the Newtonian fluids while most of the other fluids that we encounter every day belong to the class non-Newtonian fluids. This summary deals with Newtonian fluids as they are significantly more understood than non-Newtonian fluids.
