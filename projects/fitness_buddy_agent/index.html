Frequently Asked Questions

Is the bot free to use?

Yes! All commands and features are for free, including sending in messages as voice or text, compiling analyses and exporting your data. Although each interaction costs real money, every user gets a generous free quota to experiment with the agent for free for weeks!

Can I use it without voice?

Absolutely. You can also send text messages to track your workouts. Even when sending voice messages, those are not stored and only used once for processing the exercise.
